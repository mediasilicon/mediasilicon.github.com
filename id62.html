Ten Bands Digital Audio Equalizer ASIC/FPGA core (EQ-SO1)

Important Features

  • 15K gates size
  • Low power dissipation with 8 MHz clock
  • Single clock synchronous design
  • Support 48 KHz sampling rate 10 bands (31Hz, 62Hz, 125Hz, 250Hz, 500Hz, 1KHz, 2KHz, 4KHz, 8KHz, 16KHz) -20 to 20 dB digital audio equalization.
  • Straightforward interface for SoC applications

Function Overview

EQ-SO1 takes any 16 bits PCM bitstream, automatically adds gain to allocated bands. The output from the EQ-SO1 is 16  bits PCM samples with channel and PCM enabled signals.Integrate EQ-SO1 with existing audio DAC can boost all audio terminals with high quality digital equalizer.
